# Benchmark Results — v0.3.0

## Machine

- **Host**: Harvard FASRC Cluster
- **Partition**: gpu_test
- **Session**: `--mem 250G --gpus 1 --cpus-per-task 24`
- **CPU**: Intel Xeon Platinum 8358 @ 2.60GHz (24 allocated cores)
- **RAM**: 250 GB
- **Storage**: NFS (network-attached)
- **GPU**: NVIDIA A100-SXM4-40GB MIG 3g.20gb
- **Python**: 3.11.14
- **PyTorch**: 2.11.0+cu126
- **Slipstream**: 0.3.0

## Dataset

- **Source**: ImageNet-1K (s256_l512 preprocessing, JPEG q100)
- **Formats**: JPEG (~97 GB train, ~3.8 GB val), YUV420 (~173 GB train, ~6.5 GB val)
- **Loaded via**: `visionlab.datasets.load("imagenet1k", split=..., fmt=...)`

## Summary (warm epochs only, epoch 2+ averaged)

### Train (1,281,167 samples)

| Pipeline | JPEG (img/s) | YUV420 (img/s) | YUV/JPEG |
|----------|-------------|----------------|----------|
| **RRC** | **14,320** | **26,393** | **1.84x** |
| **CenterCrop** | **15,957** | **26,110** | **1.64x** |

### Val (50,000 samples)

| Pipeline | JPEG (img/s) | YUV420 (img/s) | YUV/JPEG |
|----------|-------------|----------------|----------|
| **RRC** | **14,282** | **27,502** | **1.93x** |
| **CenterCrop** | **15,957** | **26,110** | **1.64x** |

## Notes

- All benchmarks: `batch_size=256, num_threads=23, threaded mode`
- "Warm" = epochs 2+ (epoch 1 treated as warmup due to page cache cold start)
- YUV420 requires ~173 GB RAM for train set to stay fully cached; 192 GB caused page eviction and ~2x slowdown
- JPEG train fits in ~100 GB, warm by epoch 2 even at 192 GB
- Val fits easily in any allocation
